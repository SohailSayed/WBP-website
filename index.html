11% of Maize Harvested in China is Lost Because of Poor Storage

We're Solving This By Giving Small Scale Chinese Maize Farmers Access To Improved Storage Bags

Current storage solutions don't work in China.

Small scale farmers in China face unique challenges that prevent them from cutting down on storage loss.

93% Chinese Farms are Smaller Than a Hectare

The average Chinese farm is 0.65 acres, more than 270 times smaller than the average American farm.

Small Farms and Working Storage Solutions Don't Make Sense

Investing in a good storage method like Silos only pay off for large farms that produce high volumes of grain.

Chinese Farmers Canâ€™t Afford Existing Storage Solutions

Metal Silos cost 25% ($316/ton) the average Chinese Farmer's salary per ton, which wouldn't work since most farmers handle several tons of maize per harvest.

Fungus and Pests Are The Leading Cause of Storage Level Food Loss

The presence of pests and fungus near maize, traditionally stored with no external protection is the leading cause of storage level food loss. Both quality and quantity of grain are lost.

Hermetic Bags are a Low Cost, Feasible Solution.

Hermetic bags are air tight grain storage bags made from polypropylene plastic. These bags can rude maize loss to from 11% to just 1% total yield, and cost only $2.30 each.

Hermetic Bags are a Better Way of Storing Grain

Grain loss reduced by ~10.5%

The hermetic seal quickly kills insects and fungus in the grain, and the triple layer bag protects against pest infiltration.

Up to 28.27 Megatonnes Maize Could Be Saved Annually

Hermetic storage in China could save up to 28.27 mega tonnes of maize lost in small farms due to poor storage annually.

22x Cheaper Than Metal Silos

Hermetic bags cost $2.30 per 150 kg bag, which makes them more than 22x more affordable than metal silos.

Easy To Install and Use

Hermetic bags are as easy to use. There's a low barrier to entry to learning how to use the bags.
